Joseph Turner (seventh from left) served as best man for his buddy in the 1270th Engineer Combat Battalion during WWII. Weston-super-Mare, 1944. The wedding party is dressed in WWII Utility Fashion.

Nan’s research focuses on clothing use during WWII. She is currently finishing a book: Deprivation Fashion, about repurposing and innovation inspired by clothing scarcity and rationing during WWII. Readers, used to current day over-consumption and waste of clothing, will find inspiration from the conservative efforts of consumers during the 1930s and 40s to ‘Use It Up, Wear It Out, Make It Do, Or Do Without.’
